ABOUT NADIA

A performance marketer who refuses to choose between storytelling and spreadsheets—because the most powerful growth comes from mastering both.

Nadia Davis is a VP-level marketing executive with 18+ years building revenue-generating marketing engines across SaaS, DaaS, IaaS, GovTech, IT automation, healthcare technology, and data platforms. She is known for building teams from scratch, overhauling martech stacks, and creating data-driven programs that sales teams can act on immediately.

Currently VP of Marketing at CaliberMind, she leads demand generation, ABM, and marketing operations for the GTM intelligence and multi-touch attribution platform. A former CaliberMind customer herself, Nadia empowers revenue teams to harness their data for better insights, attribution, and growth.

Her career spans senior roles at PayIt, Stonebranch, LexisNexis Risk Solutions, Informa, and ND Medica, complemented by nearly a decade as an ABM and digital strategy consultant. At each stop, she designed persona-driven targeting, ran high-profile industry events, and built marketing programs that continued to deliver pipeline well beyond launch.

A former Girls in Tech board member, Nadia combines hands-on technical expertise with leadership skills to grow both teams and results.
